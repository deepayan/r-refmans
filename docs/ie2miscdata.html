ie2miscdata: Irucka Embry's Miscellaneous USGS Data Collection

Description

ie2miscdata provides various data sets [USGS Parameter codes with fixed values, USGS global time zone codes, and US Air Force Global Engineering Weather Data] created by Irucka Embry while he was a Cherokee Nation Technology Solutions (CNTS) United States Geological Survey (USGS) Contractor and/or USGS employee.
