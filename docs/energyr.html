Electric Company Rates

Description

Electric Company Financial Information as reported by FERC.

Usage

Format

Data frame with columns

Company

Electric Company

Revenue

Annual Revenue in USD

Bill

Annual Bill in USD

Year

Reporting Year

Source

http://www.ferc.gov/industries/electric/annual-charges.asp

Examples

  dim(electric)
  head(electric)
