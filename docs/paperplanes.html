Paper Planes.

Description

Distance Recordings from a Paper Plane Folding/Flying Experiment

Usage

paperplanes

Format

A data frame with eight variables: id, hour (the hour of the day), person, gender, age, plane, paper (two types: 80g and 120g), distance (recorded in meter).

Details

Visitors of a science fair (the 3. 'Nacht des Wissens' in Goettingen/Germany http://www.ndw.uni-goettingen.de/) had the opportunity to throw a paper plane – either folding one themselves or re-using an existing one.
